Elf Labs reports 42% year- over-year growth

Elf Labs' Junior Elf Fairytale Princess brand featuring, Cinderella, Snow White, Sleeping Beauty, Rapunzel, Belle, and The Little Mermaid has achieved over 42% year-over-year growth in Q2, 2023!
